• Most packages on npm, 71%, have been updated within the last year. 21% have been updated the year before that.
  • Packages that have a higher version number tend to be updated more frequently. For example, packages whose major verison is 0.* have a median of 210 days since last updated. Packages whose major version is greater than 1.* have a median of 112 days since last updated.
  • Close to a third of all packages (30%) have been updated within the last 3 months alone.
